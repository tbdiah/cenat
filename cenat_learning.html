Monitoring and Evaluation of
Tuberculosis Programs in Cambodia

Self-Paced Online Learning


STEP 1: Check Your Knowledge

Start by taking a self-assessment. Designed for practitioners responsible for the monitoring and evaluation of TB programs in Cambodia, the tool will help you identify any gaps in your skills and knowledge.


Launch Assessment

 

Step 2: Increase Your Knowledge

Self-paced online learning modules to help increase your knowledge of TB M&E.

MODULE 1: Monitoring and Evaluation of TB Programs

Time to Complete: Approximately 45 - 60 minutes

MODULE 2: TB Data Quality Assurance

Time to Complete: Approximately 30 minutes

MODULE 3: TB Data Analysis, Interpretation, and Use

Time to Complete: Approximately 60 minutes


Step 3: RE-ASSESS Your Knowledge

Now that you have finished the learning modules, feel free to re-take the self-assessment to ascertain if you have made any knowledge gains.

These resources were created by the Tuberculosis Data, Impact Assessment and Communications Hub (TB DIAH) project.

The TB DIAH project is part of USAID's performance-based measures to end TB—the Global Accelerator to End Tuberculosis. The TB DIAH website (www.tbdiah.org) features a data hub, guidance, and tools to support the work of the global TB community. TB DIAH makes possible optimal analysis and use of TB data to inform national TB programs, policies, and USAID-supported interventions.
